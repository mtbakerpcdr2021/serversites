How to buy an NFT

What is an NFT?

An NFT is a digital means of assigning and organizing rights to limited official productions of a piece of art. Similar to how many people may have copies of pictures of the Mona Lisa, but only the Louvre has the original; many people may have copies of the art in an NFT, but only one person has the official one.

So how do I buy an NFT?

The first thing you will want to do is purchase some cryptocurrency for the blockchain/coin the NFT has been released on. We have started the NFT releases on the Ethereum ERC 20 platform, then intend on expanding to other chains like binance chain as well as others in the near future. Due to this, currently you will WANT TO PURCHASE SOME ETHEREUM in order to grab one of the NFTRogs. Their are many websites that allow you to easily purchase Ethereum outright with a bank card, such as Coinbase or Swyftx.com

After your cryptocurrency is safely in your control, head over to one of the sites NFTRogs are hosted on and make your NFT purchase by following the NFT websites directions.
